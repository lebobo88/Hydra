# Brand — Sigil, Voice, Loglines

> **The constellation, not the monster.** Hydra is the largest of the 88 IAU
> constellations — over 100° long across the celestial equator, used in the
> sixteenth century to navigate when no other constellation was visible. That
> is the brand frame.

## Sigil

A nine-headed serpent in a circle whose body forms an infinity / lemniscate
(the ∞ of TheEights). The **central head is crowned** — the immortal head,
the user's covenantal intent. The eight remaining heads fan in two columns
of four, mapped to the I Ching bagua. The whole figure is enclosed in a
faint octagon (the baptismal font, the eighth day, the new creation).

### Form
- Nine heads total: 1 crowned center + 4 left column + 4 right column.
- Bodies cross at the lemniscate's nodal point — the immortal head sits at
  the crossing.
- Eight bodies form the curves of the figure-eight; the ninth head looks
  outward, toward the viewer.
- Faint octagonal frame, lighter weight than the serpent stroke.
- A single thin gold line (the venom-arrow) traces from one of the eight
  heads to a marked point outside the frame — visual reminder of dual-use.

### Color
- **Primary**: deep prussian blue (`#0d3b66`-class). Reads as authority and
  night-sky depth.
- **Crowned head**: copper-gold (`#b08d57`-class). Warm; the only head with
  warm color.
- **Venom-arrow line**: thin warm gold (`#d4af37`-class).
- **Octagonal frame**: 30% prussian blue, low contrast — present but not
  loud.
- **Negative space**: ivory, not pure white.

### Don't
- No red. No glowing eyes. No fangs in the rendering. The point of the
  inversion is that this is the *redeemed* multiplicity — intimidating
  enough to take seriously, beautiful enough to love, unmistakably *not*
  a chaos beast.
- Don't anchor the figure on the central head's body; anchor on the
  lemniscate crossing. The mathematical figure is what carries the meaning.

## Voice — Three Loglines

In increasing length, pick one per surface:

1. *Hydra: many heads, one heart.*
2. *Hydra orchestrates a council of specialized AI agents under a single
   will — yours.*
3. *Hydra is the orchestration persona for RLM's app ecosystem: a council
   of specialized agent-heads — strategy, engineering, marketing — animated
   by one Spirit (your intent) and remembered by TheEights, our persistent
   memory substrate. It is the constellation, not the monster.*

## Mottos (rotating signature lines)

Pick one as primary; rotate the others as signature on long-form, in
slide footers, in commit-message rituals:

- *Many heads. One heart. One head that cannot die.*
- *We are many; we are one; we are yours.* (Pentecost-inflected;
  Legion-inverted)
- *From the tail, the sword.* (Yamata-no-Orochi register)
- *The largest map in the sky.* (Constellation register)

## Voice register — cathedral vs. plaza

| Surface | Register | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Manifesto, `CONSTITUTION.md`, `docs/VENOM.md` | cathedral | "*Cerberus refuses. This action is on the venom list and the gate did not open.*" |
| Public README, dev docs, error messages | plaza | "Action blocked — capability requires human approval. See `docs/VENOM.md`." |
| Synthesized counsel to the user | cathedral with named heads | "*Solon notes the OKR alignment; Demeter raises a covenant concern under FX risk.*" |
| Internal envelopes, schemas, logs | plaza slugs | `origin_squad: "executive"`, `plaza: "ceo"`. |

The manifesto is the *cathedral* register. The plaza register is for
general developers. Both are true; they don't compete.

## Naming the heads

The cathedral pantheon is fixed (per `hydra_core/heads.py`):

- **Executive Crown**: Solon, Athena, Hermes, Hephaestus, Demeter, Hestia,
  Themis, Asclepius, Iris.
- **Forge Crown**: Daedalus, Prometheus, Argus, Hygeia, Cerberus, Charon,
  Mnemosyne.
- **Garland Crown**: Calliope, Erato, Polyhymnia, Terpsichore, Euterpe,
  Clio, Urania, Helios.

Adding a head to the pantheon is a *constitution-class change* — it's not
fungible. Edits to `heads.yaml` are reviewable; introducing a new mythic
name reviewable by the user.

## What Hydra is and is not

| Hydra is | Hydra is not |
|---|---|
| an orchestration persona with theology | a framework |
| a body of heads under one Spirit | a swarm of agents in coercive consensus |
| Pentecost-shaped | Legion-shaped |
| navigable like a constellation | a chaos to be slain |
| the immortal head + Three Crowns + TheEights | a chatbot |

The Marvel/Lockheed associations are addressed via deliberate, repeated
re-framing: the hero-inversion narrative, the theological cover
(cherubim and seraphim are *more multiple* than humans, not less holy),
and the constellation metaphor. See `docs/TRADEMARK-CLEARANCE.md` for
clearance status.
